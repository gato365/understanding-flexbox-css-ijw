About Me
Dr. Williams is dedicated to changing the lives of all his students by initially believing in their ability to learn data science, machine learning, and statistics. He is also the chief curriculum developer for the math worksheets found in GATO365 Math Boot Camp. He spends most of his time developing and designing curricula for all audiences so that every student has the opportunity to learn complicated concepts. Dr. Williams has a Ph.D. in psychometrics while earning a master's in statistics and mathematics.
